Whatever's In the Fridge Soup

This is a delicious soup for a cold day or just when you have a cold!

Base Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups broth (chicken, beef, or veggie)
  • 2 cups mixed veggies (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1 can beans (kidney, white, or black)
  • half cup rice, pasta, or diced potatoes

Steps to Make

  1. Heat oil in a pot. Saute onion and garlic until soft
  2. Add broth, veggies, beans, and rice/pasta/potatoes
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Bring to boil
  4. Lower to a simmer, cook 20-30 min until everything is tender
  5. Optional: Add hot sauce, squeeze of lemon, or grated chees on top.
