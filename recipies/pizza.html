Pizza

Pizza on the girll is a fantastic way to make pizza at home

Ingredients

  • Dough
    • 1 packet active dry yeast
    • 1.5 cups warm water
    • 3.5 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp sugar
  • Sauce
    • 1 can crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • .5 tsp salt
    • .5 tsp sugar (optional, if sauce tastes too acidic)
  • Toppings (adjust to taste)
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, olives, sausage
    • Fresh basil or arugula
    • Olive oil drizzle for flair

Steps to Make It

  1. Make the Dough
    1. In a large bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5-10 min until foamy.
    2. Stir in flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until shaggy dough forms
    3. Knead on a floured surface for 8-10 min until smooth and elastic
    4. Plop it in an oiled bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let it rise for 1 to 1.5 hours, until doubled in size
  2. Make the Sauce
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium
    2. Add garlic and coook for 30 seconds, then toss in the crushed tomatoes
    3. Add oregano, alst, and sugar. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally
    4. Cool it down a bit before using
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees Farenheit
    2. Punch down dough like it owes you money. Divide into two for thin crust or keep whole for thick crust
    3. Roll out into a 12-14 inch circle. Transfer to a pizza pan, stone, or inverted baking sheet
    4. Spread sauce, sprinkle cheese, and go wild with toppings
    5. Bkae for 12-15 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and crust is golden
