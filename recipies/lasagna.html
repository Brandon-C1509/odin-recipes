Lasagna

This recipe might take a little bit of work, but the results are worth it.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef or italian sausage (or half pound of both)
  • 24oz pasta sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella
  • .5 cup Parmesan
  • 1 egg (optional, helps hold the ricotta layer together)
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder

How to Make

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees farenheit
  2. Brown the meat in a pan. Drain grease, add pasta sauce, season to taste. Simmer for 5-10 minutes
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, salt, and a bit of Parmesan
  4. In a baking dish:
    • Spread a bit of sauce on the bottome
    • Layer noodles
    • Add ricotta mixture
    • Add meat sauce
    • Sprinkle mozzarellla
    • Repeat until dish is full
  5. Cover with foil. Bake 25 min. Uncover, bake 20 more min until bubbly and slightly crispy on top
  6. Let rest 10-15 min or it'll collapse.
